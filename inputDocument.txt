b.	Perceived Value
This refers to the anticipated benefit from a consumer’s perspective of a product or a service.
The key thing is; the perceived value is from the consumer’s point view. His or her feel of what 
the product offers in terms of value and in providing satisfaction and his or her feel of how much 
the prduct is worth. This is mostly depedent on emotins, tangible benefits and other intangible 
benefets associated with the product or service.


It could also be defined as the diference betwen the total obtained benefits according to the 
customer pereption and the cost that he had to pay for that. In most cases, the customer will 
buy the same product again only if he perceives to be getting some value out of the product. 
This creates a feeling of loyalty.
